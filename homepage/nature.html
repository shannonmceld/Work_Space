Discovering the Grounds

I am someone who enjoy a good hike and bike ride...

I enjoy hiking because it offers numerous physical and spiritual benefits. Being outdoors on a trail lets me enjoy nature and clean air. Hiking helps me to reduce stress, anxiety, and depression while allowing me to clear my mind and reconnect with myself. We live in a stressful world that can be hard to get away from. Social media can dominate a large part of our lives, so these outdoor activities give us a chance to step away from it all. Huntsville, Al has a National State Park that is on the mountains. I started going there to clear my heart about eleven year ago. One day I decided to walk through the park and discovered all kinds of walking and biking trails. That is where this hobby started. Over time I have looked up at different caves and trails near me that I journey through. I can say out of all my hobbies it is the most humbling.
